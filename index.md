title: Welcome to the History of Firearms in Stamps

Stamps are selected by governments to help tell unifying stories about a nation.

Postage stamps are “semantically dense” and are the “smallest icons of popular culture.” Jack Child, Miniature Messages: The Semiotics and Politics of Latin American Postage Stamps 19-21 (Durham: Duke U.Pr. 2008).


This exhibit examines American stamps related to firearms, in chronological order of the events depicted.


The exhibit also explains the particular firearms portrayed on some stamps. The firearms photos are courtesy of [NRA National Firearms Museum](https://www.nramuseum.org/). More information about particular arms is available at the Museum’s [website](https://www.nramuseum.org/).
