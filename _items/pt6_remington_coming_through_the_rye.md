Remington’s 1903 sculpture “Coming through the Rye”
depicts four cowboys having fun, as they ride around and
fire their revolvers into the air.

![Compact dark revolver with a glossy off-white grip. The cylinder has cutaways between the chambers](../img/itemimgs/colt_saa_peacemaker_1_.jpg)

The [1873 Colt Peacemaker](https://www.nramuseum.org/guns/the-galleries/the-american-west-1850-to-1900/case-21-tin-stars-sixguns-frontier-justice/colt-saa-peacemaker-(1).aspx) was a six shot revolver, and the
most iconic handgun of the American West.
