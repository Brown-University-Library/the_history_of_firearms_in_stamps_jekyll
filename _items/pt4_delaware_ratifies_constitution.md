Delaware was the first State to ratify the proposed U.S. Constitution. This bicentennial stamp was issued on July 4, 1787, followed by stamps for the bicentennials of ratification by the other 12 original States.

The man on the right appears a frontiersmen, with his legs in leather stockings. He carries an American Long rifle with its distinctive banana-shaped stock.
