The firearms in this popular souvenir sheet include the previously-discussed stamps for Annie Oakley, Geronimo, and Kit Carson. Additionally, the Overland Mail stamp depicts a stage coach and a lone horseman, both with long guns.

Not depicted with firearms, but well-known as firearms users, are mountain men Jim Bridger and Jim Beckwourth; the world-famous scout and entertainer Buffalo Bill; cowboy and showman Bill Pickett; lawmen Bat Masterson, Wyatt Earp, Charles Goodnight (Texas Ranger), Wild Bill Hickok, and Bill Tilghman; and Nez Pierce Indian leader Chief Joseph.
