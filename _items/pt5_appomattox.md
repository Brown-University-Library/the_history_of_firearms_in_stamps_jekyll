Confederate surrender at Appomattox,  Virginia, brought the Civil War to an  end. The background colors of the  stamp suggest a Union soldier (blue) looking at a stand of three surrendered Confederate rifles with bayonets (gray), and perhaps reflecting on the immense human suffering of the war. The [Barnett Model 1853 Enfield Percussion Rifle Musket](https://www.nramuseum.org/guns/the-galleries/a-nation-asunder-1861-to-1865/case-17-civil-war-imported-arms/barnett-model-1853-enfield-percussion-rifle-musket.aspx) saw much action in both armies. Here, the bayonet is detached.
