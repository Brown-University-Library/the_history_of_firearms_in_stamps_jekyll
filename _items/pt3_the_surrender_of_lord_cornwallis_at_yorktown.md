On October 19, 1781, the British army surrendered to George Washington at Yorktown, Virginia. Lord Cornwallis’s surrender was the end of the major fighting in the American Revolution, although peace was not established until the September 3, 1783, Treaty of Paris.

The souvenir sheet on a first day cover is based on a painting by Jonathan Trumbull.
