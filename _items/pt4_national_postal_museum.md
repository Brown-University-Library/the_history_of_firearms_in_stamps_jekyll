This 1993 stamp celebrates the history of the Post Office. In the foreground a Union soldier in the Civil War is writing a letter home, using a drum as a desk. In the upper left is a Pony Express rider, apparently in a hurry to get away from adversaries. The stagecoach, in the lower right, often included mail sacks in its cargo.
