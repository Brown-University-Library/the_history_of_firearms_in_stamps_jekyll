The Postal Service took the 1976 Bicentennial very seriously, issuing 113 commemorative stamps in the six years beginning in 1971.

The four-part “Rise of the Spirit of Independence” series depicted colonial communications. Here, a drummer alerts a village that the British are coming. On the left, militiamen rush into the fields. On the right, a man has hurried home for his wife to hand him his gun.
